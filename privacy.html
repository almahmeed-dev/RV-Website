Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Overview

RepVault Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

The short version: We don't collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data We Access

RepVault Pro accesses the following data on your device:

  • Motion Data: Used for automatic rep counting. Processed in real-time on your Apple Watch and never stored or transmitted.
  • HealthKit Data: With your permission, we read and write workout data to Apple Health. This data is managed by Apple's HealthKit framework and protected by Apple's privacy policies.
  • Workout History: Your exercises, sets, reps, and weights are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data to external servers

Data Storage

All workout data is stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's secure data storage frameworks. Data syncs between your devices using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework, which uses encrypted Bluetooth communication.

Third-Party Services

RepVault Pro does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your data with anyone.

HealthKit

If you choose to enable HealthKit integration, RepVault Pro will:

  • Write workout sessions to Apple Health
  • Read activity data to display in the app

This data is managed by Apple's HealthKit framework and subject to Apple's privacy policies. You can revoke these permissions at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

Data Deletion

To delete all RepVault Pro data:

  • Delete the app from your iPhone and Apple Watch
  • Optionally, delete workout data from Apple Health

Since we don't store your data externally, deleting the app removes all data permanently.

Children's Privacy

RepVault Pro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in fitness tracking.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
